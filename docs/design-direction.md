# Cecil Denny Driving School — Sun-Drenched 3D Design Direction

## Brief
- CLIENT_NAME: Cecil Denny Driving School
- NICHE: Driving Lessons
- TARGET_MARKET: London W3, learner drivers 17+
- TONE_BRIEF: Sun-drenched, Ethereal, Interactive, Premium, 3D
- GOAL: High-conversion bookings through immersive, innovative design

## Research
- Differentiation: We are moving to 3D interactivity, high-key light aesthetics, and fluid motion, which no competitors in the local market currently utilize.

## Aesthetic Direction
- Personality: Sun-Drenched 3D (Ethereal, Interactive)
- Rationale: Establishes immediate authority and differentiation by creating a digital environment that feels like a premium, sunny, architectural space rather than a brochure.

## Design System

### Colour Palette (oklch)
- PRIMARY: oklch(0.4 0.15 260) (Deep Ocean Blue)
- ACCENT: oklch(0.75 0.15 85) (Solar Yellow)
- BACKGROUND: oklch(0.99 0.005 90) (Warm Off-White)
- FOREGROUND: oklch(0.15 0.02 260) (Dark Blue/Grey)

### Typography
- DISPLAY_FONT: EB Garamond
- BODY_FONT: Outfit

### Visual Effects & Texture
- Effects: Glassmorphism, subtle film grain, soft-focus shadows.

### Interaction & Motion DNA
- Motion: Fluid 3D interactions, spring-based hover effects, scroll-linked parallax, WebGL/R3F integration for hero.

## Aesthetic Assessment
- Niche fit: 5/5
- Differentiation: 5/5
- Build complexity: High (3D interactions)
